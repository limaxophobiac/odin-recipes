Brenda's Lasagna

A cheesy lasagna

Ingredients

  • 1 package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • ½ pound shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ pund shredded Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a large point of slightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8-10 minutes.
  2. Preheat oven to 175 degrees C. Brown beef in a large skillet over medium-high heat and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Stir in spaghetti sauce and garlic and simmer for 5 minutes
  4. Combine the cheese in a bowl, stir well. Alternate layers of noodles, meet mixture and cheese mixture.
  5. Bake in oven for 30 min or untill cheese is melted.
